INTERNAL WIRE TRANSFER COMPLIANCE MEMO
Date: March 12, 2024
Subject: Large Outbound Payment Review

A highly irregular outbound wire transfer of $8.5 million has been flagged by the compliance system at First Horizon Bank. 
The funds originated from the corporate treasury account of First Horizon Bank and were deposited directly into the merchant account of Project Olympus LLC.

The authorization signature on the wire transfer form matches the handwriting of our internal VP of Finance, Evelyn Thorne. We are placing Evelyn Thorne on administrative leave pending an investigation.
